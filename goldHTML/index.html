Our Services

We provide a wide variety of tattooing services to both regular and new clients. At Hani Moriss, you can expect first-class treatment as well as 100% safe and sterile environment & equipment.

Tattooing

Tattooing is a timeless art of self-expression, where inked designs become permanent reflections of identity and creativity.

Piercing

Piercing is a bold form of self-expression, enhancing beauty and individuality through carefully placed jewelry.

Tattoo cover up

Tattoo cover-ups transform old or unwanted ink into new artwork, blending creativity and precision to give the skin a fresh start.

Tattoo design

Tattoo design is the art of creating meaningful, unique, and visually striking ink that reflects personal expression and style.

Permanent makeup

Permanent makeup enhances natural beauty with long-lasting pigments, offering effortless perfection for brows, lips, and eyeliner.

Laser removal

Laser tattoo removal fades or erases unwanted ink using advanced technology, restoring the skin with precision and care.
