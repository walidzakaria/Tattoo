FAQ

We aim to provide the best experience of using our tattoo services. We hope these frequently asked questions and their answers will help you find a solution to your problem. If not, feel free to contact us anytime.

Does getting a tattoo hurt?

Yes, but the pain level varies depending on the location and your pain tolerance. Areas with more bone or nerve endings, like ribs or wrists, tend to be more painful.

How long does a tattoo take to heal?

Most tattoos take about 2-4 weeks to heal on the surface, but full healing can take a few months. Proper aftercare is essential for a smooth healing process.

How do I take care of my new tattoo?

Keep it clean, apply a thin layer of recommended ointment, avoid scratching, direct sunlight, and swimming until it's fully healed. Follow your tattoo artist’s aftercare instructions.

Can I get a tattoo if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it’s best to consult with your tattoo artist and do a patch test if you're prone to allergic reactions.

Will my tattoo fade over time?

Yes, tattoos can fade due to sun exposure, aging, and skin regeneration. Proper aftercare and occasional touch-ups help maintain vibrancy.
